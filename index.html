Department of Operations Control (DOC)

Timeline & History

  • 132: DOC Founded by Dez to combat paranormal entities.
  • 232: Dez dies and vanishes, Board assumes control.
  • 242: Dez resurrected with anomalous powers.
  • 413: Interdimensional expansion; bases across all known realities.
  • 415: SCP Facility-422 Incident; DOC captures facility.
  • 416: Alliance formed with the O5 Council.
  • 417: PA-001 Voidsmelter contained.
  • 418: OCA-001 Virex contained.

Divisions

  • Agency of Operations (AOO): Masters of global and interdimensional coordination.
  • Paranormal Agency (PA): Specialists in supernatural anomalies.
  • Organic Capture Agency (OCA): Hunters and neutralizers of organic threats.
  • Medical Agency (MA): Research, healing, and morally ambiguous experimentation.

Entities

PA-001: Voidsmelter

Classification: Omega-Level Cosmic Anomaly

Threat Level: Absolute / Multiversal

Description: A sentient, mechanical-cosmic organism born from the death of thousands of stars, absorbing planets, light, radiation, and time itself.

OCA-001: Virex

Classification: Omega-Level Organic Anomaly

Threat Level: Extreme / Multiversal Containment Required

Description: A viscous black ink-like entity with glowing green parasites and tentacles. Controls "The Maw," an ink pocket dimension, and uses psychological warfare to subdue prey.

Protocols

Neutralization Directive (ND-001): All contained anomalies may be terminated if containment fails or poses a multiversal threat. Procedures are executed by OCA, PA, and MA under strict authorization from Dez or the Board.

After containment, all entities are informed: "You are contained. You can be killed." Reactions are logged for study.
